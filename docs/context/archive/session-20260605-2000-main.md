## Key Decisions

### 1. Name selection: Galley over Quire
A printer's galley is continuous text before it is divided into pages — an exact analog for the design where chapters are a late-bound projection, not a structural boundary in the prose file. The metaphor is strong and the App Store name appeared clear.

### 2. Rename isolated to its own commit
The mechanical rename carries zero behavior change. Isolating it from the Phase 3–4 feature commit (`510e052`) keeps bisect clean and makes the refactor trivially revertable without unwinding feature work.

### 3. Historical records not swept
ADRs and session summaries dated before the rename deliberately retain "Untitled" — they are records of decisions and work made under the prior name, not typos.
